FREQUENCY OF PUBLICATION & DATE OF RELEASE

Maiden issue

THE PUBLICATION CONTENT & USES

Presents a compilation of censuses and surveys that under went the SSRCS, which involves the substantive review of inquiries, which are sponsored and/or conducted by government agencies regardless of sample size and funding sources.

Consist of the periodic enumeration of the country's population, housing and establishments and the agriculture and fishery sector.

Provides a description of each census/survey-the conducting agency, some background information, objectives, frequency of conduct and reference period, scope and coverage, instruments, data items collected, cost, sampling design, estimation and imputation procedures, survey operations, data processing, major outputs generated, and schedule and method of dissemination. These items of information comprise the census/survey metadata, which are being made available to users to promote transparency of survey methodologies and operations.
