The Regional Technical Committee on Survey Design (RTCSD)

The Regional Technical Committee on Survey Design (RTCSD) was created thru RSCC Resolution No. 3 Series of 2004 to provide technical advice in the preparation and development of the survey designs for government censuses and sample surveys to be conducted in the region.

The technical committee is composed of professionals in survey design who will assist the NSCB-CAR in the review, evaluation and clearance of government statistical surveys or censuses in the region. The current composition of the technical committee is as follows:
