RSCC Resolution No. 6
Series of 2000

ENDORSING TO THE HONORABLE SECRETARY-GENERAL ROMULO A. VIROLA, THE
CORDILLERA REGIONAL STATISTICAL COORDINATION COMMITTEE-RECOMMENDED
CY 2001-2003 STATISTICAL BUDGET PROPOSAL OF THE NATIONAL STATISTICAL
COORDINATION BOARD-REGIONAL UNIT IN THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE
REGION (NSCB-RU-CAR) AND REQUESTING THE HONORABLE SECRETARY-GENERAL
TO INCLUDE SAID PROPOSAL INTO THE CY 2001-2003 BUDGET OF THE NATIONAL
STATISTICAL COORDINATION BOARD

WHEREAS, pursuant to the National Budget Memorandum No. 90 dated March 10, 2000, the Regional Statistical Coordination Committee in the Cordillera Administrative Region (RSCC-CAR) enjoined Regional Line Agencies to submit Statistical Budget Proposals over and above the agency baseline budgets to the RSCC-CAR;

WHEREAS, the NSCB evaluated the CY 2001-2003 Statistical Budget Proposal of the NSCB-RU-CAR to ensure consistency with Executive Order 352 or the System of Designated Statistics, the thrusts of the Philippine Statistical Development Program 1999-2004, and other national and regional concerns;

WHEREAS, the following statistical activities are needed to implement the Regional Statistical Development Program and the NSCB-RU-CAR is the proper government unit to conceptualize, obtain authorization, source out funding and implement the same:

TITLE Proposed Budget (in pesos)
2001 2002 2003
Institutionalization of Economic-Environmental
and Natural Resources Accounting in CAR		 P 702,475.20 590,675.20 629,995.20

NOW THEREFORE, on motion duly seconded, BE IT:

RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED, that the Regional Statistical Coordination Committee-CAR approves and endorses to the Honorable Secretary-General the favorable consideration of the above concerns and the inclusion of the budget of the above statistical activity of the NSCB-RU-CAR into the CY 2001-2003 Budget Proposal of the NATIONAL STATISTICAL COORDINATION BOARD.

UNANIMOUSLY APPROVED this 12th day of April 2000 at Baguio City, Philippines.
