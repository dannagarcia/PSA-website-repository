RSCC Resolution No. 5
Series of 2000

ENDORSING TO THE HONORABLE SECRETARY MANUEL A. ROXAS, THE CORDILLERA
REGIONAL STATISTICAL COORDINATION COMMITTEE-RECOMMENDED CY 2001
STATISTICAL BUDGET PROPOSAL OF THE DEPARTMENT OF TRADE AND INDUSTRY
IN THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION (DTI-CAR) AND REQUESTING THE
HONORABLE SECRETARY TO INCLUDE SAID PROPOSAL INTO THE CY 2001-2003
BUDGET OF THE DEPARTMENT OF TRADE AND INDUSTRY

WHEREAS, pursuant to the National Budget Memorandum No. 90 dated March 10, 2000, the Regional Statistical Coordination Committee in the Cordillera Administrative Region (RSCC-CAR) enjoined Regional Line Agencies to submit Statistical Budget Proposals over and above the agency baseline budgets to the RSCC-CAR Secretariat, namely the National Statistical Coordination Board Regional Unit-CAR (NSCB RU-CAR);

WHEREAS, the NSCB RU-CAR evaluated the CY 2001 Statistical Budget Proposal of the DTI-CAR to ensure consistency with Executive Order 352 or the System of Designated Statistics, the thrusts of the Philippine Statistical Development Program 1999-2004, and other national and regional concerns;

WHEREAS, the following statistical activities are needed to implement the Regional Statistical Development Program and the DTI-CAR is the proper government unit to conceptualize, obtain authorization, source out funding and implement the same:

TITLE Proposed Budget (in pesos)
2001
LGU Licensing and Databank Support Program P 885,000.00

NOW THEREFORE, on motion duly seconded, BE IT:

RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED, that the Regional Statistical Coordination Committee-CAR approves and endorses to the Honorable Secretary the favorable consideration of the above concerns and the inclusion of the budget of the above statistical activity of the DTI-CAR into the CY 2001-2003 Budget Proposal of the DEPARTMENT OF TRADE AND INDUSTRY.

UNANIMOUSLY APPROVED this 12th day of April 2000 at Baguio City, Philippines.


CERTIFIED TRUE AND CORRECT:

BENJAMIN Y. NAVARRO
Regional Unit Head, NSCB RU-CAR
Secretary, RSCC-CAR

ATTESTED:

JUAN B. NGALOB
Regional Director, NEDA-CAR
