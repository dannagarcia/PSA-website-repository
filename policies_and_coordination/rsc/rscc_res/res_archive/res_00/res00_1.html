RSCC Resolution No. 1
Series of 2000

ADOPTION OF THE 1998 RSCC WORK PROGRAM

WHEREAS, the National Statistical Coordination Board (NSCB) through Resolution No. 1, Series of 1992 and NSCB Memorandum Circular No. 01-92 created the Regional Statistical Coordination Committee (RSCC) to provide direction and guidance to regional/local statistical development activities;

WHEREAS, the RSCC was organized in the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR) on March 19, 1992 to carry out this mandate;

WHEREAS, the RSCC-CAR mandate is carried out through the implementation of an annual Work Program;

WHEREAS, the RSCC-CAR Work Program covers four major statistical activities namely:

  1. PreparatioSeptember 4, 2012/p>
  2. Assistance in setting-up the National Statistical Information Center;
  3. Provisions of data for the preparation of the macro-economic indicator system; and
  4. Facilitation and support of statistical activities;

WHEREAS, these activities, when implemented, will help address the statistical requirements for local and regional development;

WHEREAS, these four major statistical activities were translated into the detailed 1998 RSCC-CAR Work Program hereto attached and which forms part of this resolution

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED, that the Committee members approve the 1998 RSCC-CAR Work Program.

APPROVED, this 28th day of March 2000.
