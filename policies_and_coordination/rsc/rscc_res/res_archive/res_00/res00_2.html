RSCC Resolution No. 2
Series of 2000

CREATION OF THE TASK FORCE ON NSM 2000

WHEREAS, the Regional Statistical Coordination Committee (RSCC) through NSCB Resoluton NO. 1 series of 1992 can create task forces to address specific statistical issues;

WHEREAS, the celebration of the Naitonal Statistics month (NSM) aims to enhance regionwide awareness and appreciation of the value and importance of statistics to the different sectors and to elicit the support and cooperation of the public to staitstical activities;

WHEREAS, there is a need to coordinate teh various activities of teh government to ensure the success of the celebration of the National Statistics Month 2000;

WHEREAS, there is a need to operationalize this endeavor by creating a task force to formulate the work program and undertake the necessary groundwork;

WHEREAS, the RSCC-CAR shall determine the chair and the final composition of the Task Force upon issuance by the National Statistical Coordination Board (NSCB) of the CY 2000 Natinal Memorandum Circular on the National Statistics Month;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED, that the Committee create the "Task Force on NSM 2000 " to undertake the above-mentioned tasks and to initially include the following agencies/institutions: NEDA, NSCB, NSO, BAS, CPDO, and PIA.

APPROVED, this 28th day of March 2000.
