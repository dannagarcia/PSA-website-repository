ANNEX A

PROPOSED IMPLEMENTING MECHANISM FOR RETRIEVAL
OF TOURISM STATISTICS AT THE LOCAL LEVEL

The proposed scheme starts with a memorandum by the DILG Regional Office requiring the LGUs to submit tourism statistics. The Municipal Governments/City Government will then require the tourism establishments in their jurisdictions to submit data using the DOT-prescribed form and, as in the flow above, these forms are finally conveyed tot eh DOT-RO where consolidation is done. A feedback scheme, the DOT-RO will provide the LGU with the consolidated data.
