RSCC Resolution No. 4
Series of 1998

APPROVING THE MEMBERSHIP
OF PRIVATE SECTOR REPRESENTATIVES

WHEREAS, as provided for in Article II of the NSCB Resolution No. 1, Series of 1992, at leas two (2) representatives from the private sector will be identified by the Committee's government representatives;

WHEREAS, nine (9) private organizations were identified as prospective private sector representatives;

WHEREAS, out of these nine (9) private organizations, five (5) formally confirmed their interest in joining this Committee;

WHEREAS, upon initial evaluation of the Committee Secretariat, four (4) were endorsed to this body for consideration;

WHEREAS, upon the deliberatiJuly 28, 2005vate organizations will be accepted as members;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED , that the Committee include as private sector representatives the following:

  1. Philippine Chamber of Commerce and Industry - Cordillera Administrative Region (PCCI-CAR)
  2. Northern Luzon Federation of Cooperatives and Development Center, Inc. - (Norlucedec)
  3. Foster Parents Plan International (PLAN)
  4. Shountog Community Consortium for Resource Equitability in Northern Luzon (SCCORED)

APPROVED, this 20th day of May 1998.


CERTIFIED TRUE AND CORRECT:

BENJAMIN Y. NAVARRO
Regional Unit Head,
NSCB RU-CAR
Secretary, RSCC-CAR
