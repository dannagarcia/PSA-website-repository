RSCC Resolution No. 5
Series of 1998

IMPROVING TOURISM STATISTICS IN
THE CORDILLERA ADMINSTRATIVE REGION

WHEREAS, the Department of Toruism int eh Cordillera Administrative Region (DOT-CAR) is the agency concerned witht he collection of data on tourist arrivals and other tourism statistics;

WHEREAS, it is realized that the data on actual tourist arrivals is not captured due to under or non-reporting of concerned establishments to DOT-CAR;

WHEREAS, tourism statistics comprise a significant part of the REgional Development Program considering CAR's intention to become a premier ecotourism destination;

WHEREAS, as a result of RSCC deluiberations, it is currently recognized that the Local Government Units (LGUs) throuJuly 28, 2005llera Administrative Region (DILG-CAR) can contribute most in assisting DOT-CAR int his respect;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED , that the Committee request the DILG-CAR to issue a memorandum to all LGUs in CAR directing them to submit reports on tourism statistics in accordance with the guidelines and reporting shceme as attached (ANNEX A - RSCC Resolution No.5 s 1998) and which forms part of this resolution.

APPROVED, this 20th day of May 1998.
