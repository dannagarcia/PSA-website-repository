ANNEX A

REGIONAL STATISTICAL BUDGET PROPOSALS CY 1999

DTI LGU Licensing and Databank Support Program
  • Complementation with NSO & DILG MIS (forms, area coverage, provisions for database system link-up)
  • Capability-building of LGUs on MIS
DILG Data Networking through Maximum Use of Information Technology (Internet)
DAR Generation of Data on Landholdings and Land Transfer
DOT Statistics in Tourism Industry
  • Improved generation and data banking of tourism statistics (adequacy, accuracy, timeliness and disaggregation)
DSWD Poverty Mapping
  • Complementation with MBN survey
  • Should consider possible revisions in the official poverty threshold
DENR Establishment of Geographic Information System
  • complementation with GIS initiatives within the region (NEDA, CHARM) and ont eh national level NAMRIA)
MGB Establishment of Supply and Demand Data on Sand and Gravel in CAR for Research and Development
DECS Seminar-workshop on Statistical Management
  • Improvement of accuracy and retrieval rate of education statistics from local level
DOH Field Health Service Information System
  • Improvement of retrieval rate of health statistics from LGUs

The RSCC shall monitor the implementation by the agencies of the approved activities. The agencies shall be required to submit a detailed work and financial plan and periodic reports to the RSCC. Data and information outputs arising from these activities shall likewise be provided to the RSCC and be made accessible to data users.
