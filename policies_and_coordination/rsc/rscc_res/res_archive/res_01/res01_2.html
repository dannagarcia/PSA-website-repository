RSCC Resolution No. 2
Series of 2001

ENDORSING TO THE HONORABLE SECRETARY HEHERSON T. ALVAREZ THE
STATISTICAL BUDGET PROPOSAL OF THE MINES AND GEO-SCIENCES BUREAU-
CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION (MGB-CAR) AND REQUESTING THE
HONORABLE SECRETARY TO INCLUDE SAID PROPOSAL INTO THE CY 2002-2004
BUDGET OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES

WHEREAS, pursuant to the National Budget Memorandum No. 93 dated April 2, 2001, the Regional Statistical Coordination Committee in the Cordillera Administrative Region (RSCC-CAR) enjoined Regional Line Agencies to submit Statistical Budget Proposals to the RSCC-CAR Secretariat, namely the National Statistical Coordination Board Regional Unit-CAR (NSCB RU-CAR);

WHEREAS, the NSCB RU-CAR evaluated the CY 2002-2004 Statistical Budget Proposal of the MGB-CAR to ensure consistency with Executive Order 352 or the System of Designated Statistics, the thrusts of the Philippine Statistical Development Program 1999-2004, and other national and regional concerns;

WHEREAS, after the devolution of functions to LGUs on Sand and Gravel (SAG) activities, the MGB-CAR in its data production found out the low turn out of SAG permittees in the LGU and the proliferation of illegal SAG activities through out the region;

WHEREAS, the conduct of study will provide reliable data that would be beneficial to the accounting and monetary valuation of the region's sand and gravel non-metallic minerals;

WHEREAS, the study is also vital in research and development studies in the region to improve our construction industry;

WHEREAS, additional revenue generation will be expected upon proper regulation and monitoring activities of the LGUs in coordination with MGB-CAR;

WHEREAS, the mandate of MGB-CAR to promote the rational exploration, development, utilization and conservation of the region's mineral resources by establishing and installing the mechanism of database updating and management;

WHEREAS, the following statistical activity is needed to implement the Regional Statistical Development Program and the MGB-CAR is the proper government unit to conceptualize, obtain authorization, source out funding and implement the same:

TITLE Proposed Budget (in pesos)
2002 2003 2004
Establishment of Supply and Demand on Sand and Gravel in CAR 1,204,312.00 808,586.00 486,000.00

NOW THEREFORE, on motion duly seconded, BE IT:

RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED, that the Regional Statistical Coordination Committee-CAR approves and endorses to the Honorable Secretary the favorable consideration of the above concerns and the inclusion of the budgets of the above statistical activities of the MGB-CAR, amounting to TWO MILLION FOUR HUNDRED NINETY EIGHT THOUSAND EIGHT HUNDRED NINETY EIGHT PESOS (P 2,498,898.00), into the CY 2002-2004 Budget Proposal of the DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES.

UNANIMOUSLY APPROVED this 30 th day of April 2000 at Baguio City, Philippines.
