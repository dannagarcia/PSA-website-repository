Resolutions Approved by the
Regional Statistical Coordination Committee (RSCC)

  • RSCC 2006

The Regional Statistical Coordination Committee (RSCC), being the regional counterpart of the NSCB Executive Board, formulates policy measures in the form of resolutions and memorandum orders designed to introduce new statistical frameworks and activities, enhance existing methodologies, and improve the quality and accessibility of government-produced statistics in the region.
