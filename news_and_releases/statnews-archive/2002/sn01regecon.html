CAR economy grows by 0.7% in 2001

The Regional Statistical Coordination Committee-Cordillera Administrative Region (RSCC-CAR) held its first meeting for CY 2003 last April 28, 2003 at the NEDA RDC Hall, Leonard Wood Road, Baguio City attended by sixteen (16) out of the twenty-two (22) member agencies. During the said meeting, two resolutions were passed by the committee chaired by the NEDA-CAR Regional Director Juan B. Ngalob and Co-chaired by NSO Regional Director Olivia G. Gulla.

The following are the resolutions prepared and passed by the committee:

RSCC Resolution No. 1 series of 2003:

DEVELOPMENT THRUSTS OF THE STATISTICAL SYSTEM

IN THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION FOR CY 2004


RSCC Resolution No. 2 series of 2003:

ANNUAL PREPARATION OF A REGIONAL STATISTICAL CALENDAR
