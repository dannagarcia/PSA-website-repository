Calendar of releases of statistical outputs now available!

Every start of the year, the NSCB collects and consolidates the calendar of releases of statistical outputs of the different government agencies. This is a mechanism aimed at promoting transparency, timeliness and accessibility of government information by providing advance information to the public as to what and when official statistics will become available.

For this calendar year, a total of twenty-seven (27) government agencies (1 government--owned and controlled corporation, 3 local government agencies and 23 regional line agencies) comprise the regional Advance Release Calendar (ARC).

There are three (3) new agencies added in the 2008 ARC: the Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PHIC) or PhilHealth, the Office of Civil Defense (OCD) and the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC). The PRC in Baguio also provided the dates of the different professional licensure examinations scheduled for this year.
