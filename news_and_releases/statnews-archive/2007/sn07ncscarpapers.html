10th NCS will have 8 papers from CAR

Eight (8) papers from the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR) will be presented during the 10 th National Convention on Statistics (NCS) that will be held on October 1-2, 2007 at the EDSA Shangri-La Hotel, Mandaluyong City.

The paper entitled “ The Characteristics of Selected Micro -Entrepreneurial Ventures: A Vital Tool Towards Success ,” authored by Ms. Lorraine B. Ngaosi and Gladys M. Navarro , faculty members of the Saint Louis University, Baguio City, is one of the three invited papers that will be presented under the Trade & Industry Statistics session. Click on the link below to view the abstract of the paper:

http://www.nscb.gov.ph/ncs/10thNCS/abstracts/Invited/49%20Trade/10thNCS_Abstract_LBNgaosiGMNavarro.pdf

The seven (7) contributed papers will be presented under the following sessions: environmental and natural resources statistics, business statistics, education statistics, health, nutrition and biostatistics, and poverty statistics.

    The NCS is being conducted every three years by the Philippine Statistical System (PSS), spearheaded by the National Statistical Coordination Board (NSCB), to: 1) provide a forum for practitioners and theoreticians in the field of statistics to discuss prevailing issues and emerging development s affecting the PSS; and 2) elicit the cooperation and support of statisticians and professionals in related fields from the government, academe and private sector towards a more responsive statistical system.
