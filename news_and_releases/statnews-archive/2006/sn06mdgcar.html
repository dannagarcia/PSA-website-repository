MDG indicators for Cordillera now online

The data sets of the Millennium Development Goals (MDG) of CAR are now online! The compilation of the indicators is a joint effort of the regional line agencies and local government units in the region. The NSCB-CAR will maintain a database and update the regional MDG web pages. Click on the MDG button at the right links panel to access the MDG data sets for CAR.
