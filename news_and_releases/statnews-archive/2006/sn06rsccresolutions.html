RSCC-CAR passes two resolutions

The Regional Statistical Coordination Committee - Cordillera Administrative Region (RSCC-CAR) held a meeting last December 22, 2006 at the RDC Hall, NEDA, Baguio City. Assistant Regional Director Leon M. Dacanay, Jr. of NEDA-CAR chaired said meeting which was attended by 16 representatives from member-agencies and 5 resource persons for a total of 21 participants. RSCC-CAR passed two resolutions:

RSCC Resolution No. 5 series of 2006:
Integration of Disaster Risk Management in Statistical Activities

RSCC Resolution No. 6 series of 2006:
Adoption of the 2007 Work Program of the Task Force on Disaster Management Statistics
