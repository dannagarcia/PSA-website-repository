2012 Regional Social and Economic Trends for CAR in CD-ROM now available

The National Statistical Coordination Board (NSCB) Regional Division-CAR announces the release of the 2012 edition of the Regional Social and Economic Trends (RSET) for the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR).

The RSET-CAR, now on its 12th edition, has proven to be very useful for planning and decision-making and for setting policies towards effective governance and improved service delivery at the local level. The RSET-CAR presents annual time series data on various sectors of the region and its provinces, cities, and municipalities. It also contains data that are disaggregated in different dimensions and includes a glossary of terms of official concepts and definitions for statistical purposes as approved by the NSCB.

This edition of the RSET-CAR features the most recent population count for the region by province, city and municipality based on the results of the 2010 Census of Population and Housing conducted by the National Statistics Office (NSO); 2009 poverty statistics for the basic sectors estimated by the NSCB; and the latest Human Development Index (HDI) of the Cordilleras jointly released by the NSCB and the Human Development Network (HDN).

The 2012 RSET-CAR can now be availed of in CD-ROM.

BENJAMIN Y. NAVARRO
Head, NSCB-CAR
