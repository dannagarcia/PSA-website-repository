News Conference on the 2008 Economic Performance of Cordillera slated July 22

The National Statistical Coordination Board (NSCB) Regional Division CAR announces the holding of a news conference on the performance of the economy of the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR) for the year 2008 with BENJAMIN Y. NAVARRO, Head of NSCB-CAR presenting the highlights of the regional economy and DIRECTOR JUAN B. NGALOB of NEDA-CAR as resource person. The news conference will be held on July 22, 2009 (Wednesday), 1:30 p.m, at the Marco Polo Function Room, El Cielito Inn, Baguio City.

(Signed)
ROMULO A. VIROLA
Secretary General
