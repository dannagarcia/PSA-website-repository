MDG Watch

The MDG Watch is an infosheet prepared by the National Statistical Coordination Board (NSCB) showing the progress of the nation, region, province, or municipality based on Millennium Development Goals (MDG) indicators.

The infosheet presents the MDG indicators that are available, baseline line data, target rates/goal for each indicator, the latest data and the probability of achieving each target/goal.
