The Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA) will be conducting the 9th National Convention for Solemnizing Officers (9NCSO) with the theme “Get Every Solemnizing Officer in the Picture”. This will be held at the SMX Convention Center, SM Mall of Asia, Pasay City on August 25-27, 2015.

We are pleased to invite you and your City/Municipal Civil Registrar to the said convention. The convention aims to provide a venue for exchange of ideas and sharing experiences for the solemnizing officers, city/municipal civil registrars, mayors, members of judiciary and other stakeholders. Updates on marriage laws and marriage registrations and the role and experiences of solemnizing officers in relation to marriage laws and procedures are only few among the topics to be presented during the convention.

May we also invite and encourage participants, particularly the solemnizing officers to propose papers (for exhibit purposes) documenting best practices on any of the related topics as mentioned above as applicable in their respective LGUs.

A registration fee of Php 3,600.00 will be collected from the participants to cover the cost of venue, food (AM/PM snacks and lunch) and the convention kit. The accommodation shall be to the account of the participant. Should you need additional information on the convention and the exhibit papers, please feel free to contact Ms. Editha R. Orcilla, Chairman of the Program and Invitation Committee at telephone 926-1075, email address at E.Orcilla@psa.gov.ph. You may also inquire other details from the PSA Regional Director or Provincial Statistics Officers in your respective areas.
