Regional Statistical Services Office (RSSO)



The Regional Statistical Services Office (RSSO) and Provincial Statistical Offices (PSO) established in the Cordillera Administrative Region shall:

  • Provide technical staff support to the PSA;
  • Provide technical assistance as may be required by the implementing agencies and local governments in the regions and provinces; and
  • Perform such other tasks as may be assigned by the National Statistician.

Statistical Operations and Coordination Division Functions

  1. Responsible in the statistical, technical and ICT operations as well as coordination of the different activities of the RSSO/PO.
  2. Responsible in all statistical coordination and development activities in the region:
    1. Plan, implement and coordinate statistical activities for the development of the sub -national statistical system;
    2. Formulate, implement and monitor statistical policies and programs at the sub-national level;
  3. 3. Adopt and implement statistical coordination mechanisms, statistical standards and classification systems at the sub-national level;
  4. Provide technical assistance or statistical services to local statistical coordination committees, interagency committees, local line agencies, and the academe;
  5. Prepare, produce and disseminate local statistical publications like the Regional Social and Economic Trends, Countryside in Figures, Statistical Handbook on Women and Men as well as local statistics on poverty, economic accounts and other local-level statistics;
  6. Review and assess statistical methodologies used in the generation and dissemination of local level statistics.
  7. Manage and maintain the PSA Statistical Information Center, including the website, at Regional Level;
  8. Compile, maintain and update database on statistical data at the subnational level;
  9. Recommend measures to develop/strengthen the capability of local government units (LGUs), local line agencies in the production, analysis, utilization and dissemination of local data for local development planning and project/program monitoring;

Civil Registration and Administrative Support Division Functions

  1. Responsible for the civil registration and vital statistics data and services and coordinating activities in the region.
  2. Responsible in the operation of CRS outlets in the region, including customer services and other civil registration concerns.
  3. Responsible for the general administration, financial services, human resource management, human resource development and procurement activities of the RSSO and the POs.
