Provincial Statistical Office (PSO)



The Provincial Statistical Offices (PSO) established in each of the provinces shall:

  • Provide technical staff support to the PSA;
  • Provide technical assistance as may be required by the implementing agencies and local governments in the provinces; and
  • Perform such other tasks as may be assigned by the National Statistician.

Provincial Statistical Office Functions

  1. Undertakes census and survey operations in the province and processes census and survey questionnaires (including data validation and review)
  2. Undertakes preparatory activities for census and survey operation- training, recruitment, coordination, forms and materials, strategies, info campaign(tri-media), budget preparation; workload preparation
  3. Collects, processes and generates/submits statistics and other administrative-based and data files for the province;
  4. Formulates local strategies for the efficient conduct of activities in the province;
  5. Prepares and disseminates press/special releases on selected statistics, civil registration and other office activities;
  6. Represents the PSA in inter-agency meetings, conferences , fora, and other similar functions in the province;
  7. Reviews and compiles barangay maps during census/survey operations;
  8. Coordinates with and provides technical assistance to LCROs/LGUs on statistical and civil registration matters and other activities in the province;
  9. Manages the operation of the frontline service counter/outlet for civil registry services in the provincial office;
  10. Evaluates and recommends applications for CRASM of Solemnizing Officers in the province;
  11. Conducts information campaign on civil registration, organizes/assists in the conduct of relevant trainings/seminars, and provides technical support and supervision to LCROs in the province;
  12. Manages and maintains the Information Technology in the Province
  13. Preparation and implementation of work and financial plan (WFP) of the province
  14. Prepares and submits periodic financial reports, regular/special reports on statistical, civil registration and administrative activities of the provincial office;
  15. Processes and pays financial claims filed in the provincial office;
  16. Manage and maintain the PSA Statistical Information Center and website at the provincial level;
  17. Plans and implements for efficient conduct of statistical activities in the province (NSM, NCS, PSQ)
  18. Implementation of Census Compliance Project
  19. Assist in the implementation of statistical coordination mechanisms, statistical standards and classification systems at the sub-national level
