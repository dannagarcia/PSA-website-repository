RSCC Resolution No. 3
Series of 1999

CREATION OF THE TASK FORCE ON INVESTMENTS

WHEREAS, the Regional Statistical Coordination Committee (RSCC) through NSCB Resolution NO. 1 series of 1992 can create task forces to address specific statistical issues;

WHEREAS, the persistent problem of investment statistics was recognized by this Committee since 15 April 1998 when it issued RSCC Resolution No. 03-98 endorsing to the Cordillera Development Council (CDC) the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) proposal on LGU Licensing and Databank Support Program;

WHEREAS, there is a need to improve investment statistics so as to have aSeptember 4, 2012>

WHEREAS, there is a need to operationalize this endeavor by creating a task force to formulate the necessary work program and undertake the essential groundwork;

NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED, that the Committee create the "Task Force on Investments " to undertake the above-mentioned tasks and to be composed of the following:

  • DTI - Chairman
  • DILG- Member
  • NEDA - Member
  • NSCB - Member
  • NSO - Member
  • SEC - Member

APPROVED, this 24 th day of August 1999.
