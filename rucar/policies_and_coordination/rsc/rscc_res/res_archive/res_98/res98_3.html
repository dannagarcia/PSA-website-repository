RSCC Resolution No. 3
Series of 1998

ENDORSING THE STATISTICAL BUDGET PROPOSALS
OF THE REGIONAL LINE AGENCIES IN THE CORDILLERA

WHEREAS, the Regional Statistical Coordination Committee was created through NSCB Resolution No. 1 and the NSCB Memorandum Circular No. 01-92 to serve as the highest policy making and coordinating body on statistical matters and to provide direction and guidance to the regional/local statistical development activities;

WHEREAS, the National Statistical Coordination Board Regional Unit in the Cordillera Administrative Region (NSCB RU-CAR) was created to address the concerns raised by the Cordillera Development Council in ResoSeptember 4, 201236">WHEREAS, the NSCB RU-CAR by virtue of Regional Budget Memorandum No 01-98 was tasked to review the statistical budget proposals of the regional line agencies in the Cordillera;

WHEREAS, statistical budget proposals of the regional line agencies in the Cordillera are as follows:

AGENCY TITLE OF ACTIVITY
DTI LGU Licensing and Databank Support Program
DILG Data Networking through Maximum Use of Information Technology (Internet)
DAR Generation of Data on Landholdings and Land Transfer
DOT Statistics in Tourism Industry
DSWD Poverty Mapping
DENR Establishment of Geographic Information System
MGB Establishment of Supply and Demand Data on Sand and Gravel in CAR for Research and Development
DECS Seminar-workshop on Statistical Management
DOH Field Health Service Information System

WHEREAS, these statistical budget proposals have been reviewed by the NSCB RU-CAR and are favorably endorsed subject to the conditions set in its assessment (see ANNEX: RSBR Form 02);

WHEREAS, these proposals, when approved, will generate or improve new generation of the designated statistical activities or data series as mandated under E. O. 352 and/or the appropriate NSCB Resolutions;

WHEREAS, these proposals when approved, are in support of the establishment of a well-coordinated local statistical system as mandated by EO 135;

WHEREAS, these proposals, when approved and implemented, will assist in he statistical development of the region;

NOW, THEREFORE, on motion duly seconded, BE IT RESOLVED AS IT IS HEREBY RESOLVED , that the RSCC endorse the statistical budget proposals of the regional line agencies in the Cordillera to the Joint CEB/CRA acting as the Cordillera Development Council (CDC).

UNANIMOUSLY APPROVED, this 15th day of April1998 at Baguio City, Philippines.
