Library Services

The National Statistical Information Center (NSIC) in the Cordillera Administrative Region collects and builds up its library holdings and these can be accessed by any walk-in client of the NSIC Library.

The NSIC Library contains a wide collection of local and international statistical publications and reference materials on various concerns; provides updates on major social and economic indicators; and offers a publications exchange program. Walk-in customers are free to use the library from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. with no noon break, Mondays to Fridays.
