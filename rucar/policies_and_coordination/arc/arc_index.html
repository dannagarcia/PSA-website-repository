Agency Release Calendar (ARC)

  • ARC

The ARC is a mechanism aimed at promoting transparency, timeliness and accessibility of government information by providing advance information to the public as to what and when official statistics will become available. The NSCB consolidates the statistical outputs/data produced in the region pursuant to RSCC Resolution Number 01 series of 2004.

The calendar of releases is sorted by AGENCY and by the MONTH of release. Click on the drop-down menus at the right to view the ARCs by agency or by month of release.
