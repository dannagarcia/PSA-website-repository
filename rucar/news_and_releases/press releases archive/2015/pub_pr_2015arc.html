2015 Agency Release Calendar for CAR is now available

The consolidated 2015 Agency Release Calendar (ARC) for the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR) can now be accessed online. The ARC is the schedule of release of statistical outputs of selected government agencies within the region. For each source agency, the ARC contains information on what statistical data are available, period covered, level of disaggregation, schedule of data release, form of output (e.g. print, e-copy and/or web download), as well as the focal person and telephone number and/or email address. For this year, a total of 24 government agencies submitted their respective ARC.
