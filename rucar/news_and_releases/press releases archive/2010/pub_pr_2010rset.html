2011 Regional Social and Economic Trends for CAR in CDROM Now Available

The National Statistical Coordination Board (NSCB) Regional Division CAR announces the release of the 2011 edition of the Regional Social and Economic Trends (RSET) for the Cordillera Administrative Region.

The RSET continues to be the most comprehensive annual statistical publication in the region covering 17 sectors featuring over 200 statistical tables. The yearly compilation of the key economic and social indicators is the result of the coordination of the office with the various local and national government agencies. The preparation of the publication is one of our principal efforts in information dissemination and in meeting the data needs for research, policy-making and development planning.

The 2011 RSET-CAR can now be availed of in CDROM version.

BENJAMIN Y. NAVARRO
Head, NSCB-CAR
