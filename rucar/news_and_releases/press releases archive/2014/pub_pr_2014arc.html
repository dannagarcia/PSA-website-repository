2014 Agency Release Calendar for CAR is now online

The consolidated 2014 Agency Release Calendar (ARC) for the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR) can now be accessed online. The ARC is the schedule of statistical outputs of selected government agencies. For every source agency, the ARC contains information on what statistical data are available, period covered, level of disaggregation, schedule of data release, form of output (e.g., print, e-copy and/or web download), as well as the contact person and telephone number(s).

The 2014 ARC of the region can be accessed at the ARC section on the Philippine Statistics Authority (NSCB-CAR) website http://www.nscb.gov.ph/rucar/arc.htm. For easy reference and navigation, drop-down menus are provided for each agency release calendar and for each month of the year.

The complete 2014 ARC document can be downloaded in two (2) PDF files: release calendar sorted by agency (http://www.nscb.gov.ph/rucar/pdf/arc/2014ARCby agency.pdf) and release calendar sorted by month of release (http://www.nscb. gov.ph/rucar/pdf/arc/2014 ARC by month.pdf ).

The ARC is prepared annually by the PSA-NSCB to promote transparency, timeliness, and accessibility of government statistics.
