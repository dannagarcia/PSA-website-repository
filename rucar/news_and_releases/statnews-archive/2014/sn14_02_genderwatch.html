PSA releases 2014 Gender Watch for the Cordilleras

In support of the national Women’s Month celebration, the Philippine Statistics Authority - National Statistical Coordination Board (PSA-NSCB) released the 2014 Gender Watch: Women and Men in the Cordillera Administrative Region. This issue was prepared by the PSA-NSCB unit in CAR. This year’s theme is Juana, ang Tatag Mo ay Tatag Natin sa Pagbangon at Pagsulong!

The statistical compendium features relevant sex-disaggregated statistics in the following areas of concern: population and families, work, agriculture and agrarian reform, education, health and nutrition, social welfare, public life, migration and peace and human rights. Moreover, it includes indicators on gender development and gender equality.

The 2014 Gender Watch is now available online and may be downloaded at www.nscb.gov.ph/rucar.
