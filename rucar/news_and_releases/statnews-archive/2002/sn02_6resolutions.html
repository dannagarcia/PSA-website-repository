Six (6) Resolutions Passed in the 1st Meeting of the RSCC-CAR

The Regional Statistical Coordination Committee-Cordillera Administrative Region (RSCC-CAR) held its first meeting for CY 2002 last April 22 at the NEDA RDC Hall, Leonard Wood Road, Baguio City attended by fifteen (15) out of the twenty-two (22) member agencies. During the said meeting, six (6) resolutions were ratified by the committee chaired by the Regional Director of the NEDA-CAR, RD Juan B. Ngalob.

The following are the resolutions prepared and passed by the committee:

RSCC Resolution No. 1 series of 2002:

ENDORSING THE CONDUCT OF THE PROJECT ON LOCAL HEALTH ACCOUNTS IN THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION


RSCC Resolution No. 2 series of 2002:

REQUESTING THE GOVERNMENT AND PRIVATE OFFICES TO PROVIDE TO THE NATIONAL STATISTICAL COORDINATION BOARD (NSCB) REGIONAL UNIT-CAR DATA NEEDED FOR THE ESTIMATION OF THE GROSS REGIONAL DOMESTIC PRODUCT OF THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION (CAR)


RSCC Resolution No. 3 series of 2002:

ENDORSING TO THE MUNICIPALITIES OF THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION THE ADOPTION OF THE BUSINESS PERMIT APPLICATION FORM AND SYSTEM OF THE MUNICIPALITY OF TUBA, BENGUET (Draft copy)


RSCC Resolution No. 4 series of 2002:

ENDORSING TO THE MUNICIPALITIES OF THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION THE ADOPTION OF TOURISM COMPLIANCE CERTIFICATE OF THE DEPARTMENT OF TOURISM-CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION (Draft copy)


RSCC Resolution No. 5 series of 2002:

ENDORSING THE APPROVAL AND ADOPTION OF THE PROJECT OPERATIONALIZING A QUICK RESPONDING REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT INDICATORS SYSTEM FOR PLAN IMPLEMENTATION MONITORING OF THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION


RSCC Resolution No. 6 series of 2002:

ENDORSING TO THE HONORABLE SECRETARY HEHERSON T. ALVAREZ, THE CORDILLERA REGIONAL STATISTICAL COORDINATION COMMITTEE-RECOMMENDED CY 2003-2005 STATISTICAL BUDGET PROPOSAL OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES IN THE CORDILLERA ADMINISTRATIVE REGION (DENR-CAR) AND REQUESTING THE HONORABLE SECRETARY TO INCLUDE SAID PROPOSAL INTO THE CY 2003-2005 BUDGET OF THE DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES
