Local Health Accounts (LHA) Project

The Department of Health (DOH) is implementing the Integrated Community Health Services Project (ICHSP) which is partly funded through a loan from the Asian Development Bank (ADB) and a grant from the Australian Agency for International Development (AUSAID). A sub-component of the ICSHP is the Health Care Financing System is partially designed to provide assistance in the development of Local Health Accounts (LHA) in the following project provinces:

  • Apayao
  • Kalinga
  • Guimaras
  • Palawan
  • Surigao del Norte
  • South Cotabato

The DOH sought the technical assistance from the NSCB to undertake development and implementation activities of the LHA since the NSCB has the mandate and is equipped with the needed technology, human and other resources to carry out the project.

GENERAL OBJECTIVE

To develop a local health accounts system

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES
  1. To develop the capability of the DOH field personnel, Provincial Governments and the NSCB Regional Units in the compilation of the local health accounts;
  2. To formulate a scientific tool for the planning, formulation and assessment of provincial health care policies, services and financing; and
  3. To develop the local data system using the local health accounts as framework.
EXPECTED OUTPUTS
  1. Updated LHA for 1999-2000 for the pilot provinces;
  2. Sources and Methods Manual for the compilation of the LHA;
  3. Recommendations for the institutionalization of the LHA compilation/system;
  4. Upgraded statistical capability of the DOH, Provincial Governments and the NSCB; and
  5. Users' fora.
IMPORTANCE OF HEALTH ACCOUNTS
  • Provides insights on the efficiency and effectiveness of health care financing
  • Helps determine appropriate interventions to improve delivery of health care
