• Problems and Solutions

Problems and solutions on your civil registry documents.

Birth Certificate
First Name
  1. Blurred
  2. Wrong Spelling
  3. No First Name
  4. First name used is different from the first name entered in the birth certificate
  5. First name is “Baby Boy”, “Baby Girl”, “Boy”, “Girl”, “Baby”
Last Name
  1. Blurred
  2. Wrong Spelling
  3. No Last Name
  4. Illegitimate Child
Middle Name
  1. Blurred
  2. Wrong Spelling
  3. No Middle Name
  4. Different from the middle name entered in the birth certificate
  5. Middle names of the child and the mother in the birth certificate are wrong
  6. Interchanged middle and last name
  7. Compound middle names like Dela Cruz, Quintos Deles, Villa Roman
  8. Middle initial is entered in the birth certificate instead of the full middle name
Marriage Certificate
  1. Wrong spelling on the name of bride and/or groom
  2. No entries in some of the items in the Certificate of Marriage
  3. Blurred/unreadable entries
  4. Negative result or No record at NSO
  5. Error in the date and place of marriage
  6. Annotation on the annulment/declaration of nullity of marriage
  7. Annotation on the effects of divorce declared in a foreign country
  8. No signatures of the contracting parties (Replacement of NSO copy based on CTC from local)
  9. No signatures/different signatures of issuing officer from concerned LCRO
Death Certificate
  1. Error in the first name of the deceased in the Certificate of Death (COD)
  2. Error in the date and place of Death
  3. Blurred/unreadable entries
  4. No entries in some items
  5. Negative results at NSO
  6. No signatures/different signatures of issuing officer from concern LCRO
