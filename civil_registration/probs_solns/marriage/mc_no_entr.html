No entries in some of the items in the Certificate of Marriage

The COM lacks entry/ies in any of the items except at the Certification portion.

How to correct

A supplemental report may be filed at the LCRO where the marriage certificate was registered. Requirements of filing a supplemental report at the LCRO is the Affidavit of Supplemental Report on missing entries executed by the owner of the document and the copy of COLB from NSO.
