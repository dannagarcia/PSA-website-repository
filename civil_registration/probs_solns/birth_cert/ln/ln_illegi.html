Illegitimate Child

Born before August 3, 1988
  1. If acknowledged by both parents
  2. If acknowledged by only one parent
  3. Child under the surname of the mother and acknowledged by the father through authentic writing
Born after August 3, 1988
  1. Birth certificate already registered and child is under the surname of the mother and the father executed an affidavit of acknowledgment
  2. Birth certificate already registered and child is under the surname of the mother and a Private Handwritten Instrument (PHI) by the father is presented
  3. Birth certificate is not yet registered and the father acknowledges the child at the back of the birth certificate or in a separate public instrument
